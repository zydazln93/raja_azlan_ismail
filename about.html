About

GEOSCIENCE ANALYTICS SERVICES provides specialized technical services in operations geology,sedimentology, and stratigraph - key disciplines that support risk reduction and enhance efficiency across oil and gas exploration, development, and production activities.

With nearly 40 years of industry experience, the company has contributed to projects across several major geological basins, including the Malay Basin, North Sabah, Sarawak, and the Arabian Basin in the Middle East. This extensive regional exposure brings deep technical insight into subsurface characterization and reservoir behavior.

Our mission is to deliver high-quality geological solutions that focus on understanding, characterizing, and managing hydrocarbon reservoirs safely and effectively.

We bring strong expertise in:

  • Operations geology, including wellsite work, well planning, formation evaluation, pore pressure analysis, and geosteering support.
  • Sedimentology, with emphasis on reservoir characterization and depositional environment interpretation.
  • Stratigraphy, covering sequence stratigraphy, climate stratigraphy, and well log correlation.
