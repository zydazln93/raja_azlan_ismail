About Us

GEOSCIENCE ANALYTICS SERVICES provides specialized technical services in operations geology, sedimentology, and stratigraph - key disciplines that support risk reduction and enhance efficiency across oil and gas exploration, development, and production activities.

With nearly 40 years of industry experience, the company has contributed to projects across several major geological basins, including the Malay Basin, North Sabah, Sarawak, and the Arabian Basin in the Middle East. This extensive regional exposure brings deep technical insight into subsurface characterization and reservoir behavior.

Our mission is to deliver high-quality geological solutions that focus on understanding, characterizing, and managing hydrocarbon reservoirs safely and effectively.

We bring strong expertise in:

  • Operations geology, including wellsite work, well planning, formation evaluation, pore pressure analysis, and geosteering support.
  • Sedimentology, with emphasis on reservoir characterization and depositional environment interpretation.
  • Stratigraphy, covering sequence stratigraphy, climate stratigraphy, and well log correlation.

Our Services

Rock Cuttings Digitalization

High-resolution imaging and systematic digital archiving of rock cuttings to enhance geological interpretation workflows. This service improves data accessibility, consistency, and long-term preservation of physical samples.

Operations Geology Support

End-to-end geological support for drilling activities, including wellsite geology, formation evaluation, real-time monitoring, pore pressure interpretation, and well planning. This ensures safer drilling operations and informed decision-making.

Sedimentology & Reservoir Characterization

Detailed analysis of sedimentary facies, depositional environments, and reservoir quality to support field development planning. This includes integrating core, cuttings, and log data for high-confidence reservoir interpretations.

Stratigraphy & Well Log Correlation

Comprehensive sequence stratigraphy, climate stratigraphy, and multi-well log correlation studies to establish robust subsurface frameworks and support exploration-to-development workflows.

Geosteering Services

Real-time geosteering and trajectory adjustment support to optimize well placement within target reservoirs. This includes structural interpretation, geologic modeling updates, and lateral well monitoring.
